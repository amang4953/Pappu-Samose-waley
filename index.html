Our Story

A Journey of Flavor,
Legacy & Innovation

Since 2006, Pappu Samose Waley has been woven into the very fabric of Kanpur's food culture. What began as a modest snack stall on the bustling Birhana Road has blossomed into one of the city's most recognizable culinary landmarks — a place where generations of families have gathered, students have celebrated, and food enthusiasts have discovered something truly special.

The brand became famous not only because of its traditional samosas, but because of its audacious spirit of innovation. By introducing bold new flavors like Pizza Cheese Samosa, Makhani Paneer Samosa, Chocolate Samosa, and the beloved Khoya Samosa, Pappu Samose Waley created an identity that stands apart from every ordinary snack shop.
